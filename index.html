About AMU DRIVE

Asif Kamal - Founder & CEO

About the CEO

Asif Kamal is the Founder & CEO of AMU Drive, a visionary digital initiative built to serve both aspiring candidates seeking admission to Aligarh Muslim University and current AMU students. Recognizing the challenges students face in accessing structured and reliable information, he established AMU Drive as a centralized academic support platform.

His vision is to create an inclusive digital ecosystem where AMU aspirants can access clear admission guidance and free preparation resources, while enrolled students can benefit from organized study materials, timely updates, and essential academic support.

Driven by a commitment to accessibility and transparency, he believes that education should be supported by equal access to information. Through innovation and a student-first mindset, he aims to position AMU Drive as a trusted companion throughout every stage of the AMU journey from preparation to academic achievement.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower AMU aspirants and students by providing a reliable, organized, and completely free academic support platform. We are dedicated to delivering authentic admission guidance, structured study materials, and timely university updates in one accessible digital space.

AMU Drive strives to reduce confusion, promote equal access to information, and support students in achieving their academic goals with clarity and confidence. Through a student-first approach and continuous innovation, we aim to build a platform that genuinely contributes to academic success and growth.
